Step 1: Place Tips

You can place tips on:

  • Each match, including the knockout phase
  • Specials, e.g. the outright winner

Tips on the specials can be placed up to one minute before the opening game.

Tips on matches can be placed up to one minute before the match starts.

Important Tips are always placed on the summarized result, including all goals shot in overtime or penalty shoot-out.

Step 2: Sit Back and Collect Points

You receive the following points as soon as a match has finished:

  • 3 points: For the correct result
  • 2 points: For the correct goal difference (including ties)
  • 1 point: For the correct winner
  • 0 points: If none of the above applies

The points for the special tips are awarded after the tournament.

You are ranked globally, with your friends and within all pools you joined.
